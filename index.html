About Ozone Travel

Bridging cultures and creating unforgettable travel experiences since 2010

Our Story

Founded in 2010 in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia, and later opened a branch in Arlington, Virginia. Ozone Travel and Tour has grown to become a trusted name in international travel services.

We specialize in creating customized travel experiences that combine cultural immersion, adventure, and comfort. Our deep connections in both the U.S. and Ethiopia allow us to offer unique insights and access to destinations.

Our team of travel experts brings together decades of experience in tour operations, flight bookings, and logistical coordination to ensure every journey is seamless.

Our Mission

Our mission is to be the leading and most reliable travel management company USA and Ethiopia, setting the benchmark for professionalism and dependability. We are committed to delivering exceptional value and timely travel solutions to our clients by offering the best available deals and opportunities.

Our Vision

Our vision is to excel in the travel industry—both domestically and internationally—by providing high-quality services with a strong emphasis on loyalty, integrity, and customer satisfaction.

Our Values

Integrity, Cultural Respect, Customer Focus, Innovation, and Community Engagement guide everything we do.
