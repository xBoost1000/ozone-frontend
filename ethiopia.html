Ozone offers immersive private Ethiopia tours ranging from 1 to 9 days. Whether you're short on time or seeking a detailed journey, our expert-led tours include transportation, hotels, guided sightseeing, and cultural immersion.

Our Ethiopia Tour Packages

1 Day

Ethiopia Essentials Tour

1 Day Addis Ababa Tour

Experience Ethiopia's capital in a single enriching day. Take in panoramic views from Entoto Hills, visit the National Museum to meet Lucy, explore historic cathedrals and vibrant markets, and enjoy an authentic Ethiopian coffee ceremony.

Itinerary Highlights:

  • Entoto Hills & Holy Trinity Cathedra
  • National Museum & Unity Park
  • Merkato Market
  • Coffee Ceremony.

3 Days

Eastern Ethiopia and Harar Tour

3 Days Eastern Ethiopia and Harar Tour

Explore the Islamic heritage and poetic history of Harar. Visit the 16th-century walled city, bustling markets, and historical museums. Watch the famous "Hyena Man" feeding ritual. Conclude with a visit to the National Museum in Addis and a farewell cultural dinner.

Itinerary Highlights:

  • Harar Jugol & Islamic Markets
  • Hyena Feeding Show
  • Arthur Rimbaud House
  • National Museum

4 Days

Northern Historical Tours

6 Days Northern Historical Tour

Uncover the cradle of ancient empires. Start in Addis Ababa before flying to Lalibela to explore the magnificent rock-hewn churches. Continue to Axum to witness towering obelisks and the legendary Queen of Sheba's palace. Discover Yeha Temple and Debre Damo Monastery. Conclude with a scenic drive through Gheralta Mountains and explore the cliff-side Abune Yemata Church.

Itinerary Highlights:

  • Lalibela Churches
  • Axum Obelisks & Queen of Sheba Relics
  • Yeha Temple & Debre Damo
  • Abune Yemata Hike
Book Now

5 Days

Western Ethiopia Nature & Coffee Trail

7 Days Western Ethiopia Nature & Coffee Trail

Venture into Ethiopia's lush rainforest and coffee heartland. Begin in Addis Ababa with a city tour. Trek or horseback ride around Wenchi Crater Lake, visit Jimma and the palace of Ras Abba Jifar II, and explore the wild Bonga region. Discover wetlands, waterfalls, honey production, and meet spiritual leaders of the Kaffa region.

Itinerary Highlights:

  • Wenchi Crater Lake
  • Jimma Royal Palace
  • Kaffa Biosphere & Barta Waterfall
  • Traditional Honey Farms
Book Now

9 Days

Southern Ethiopia Cultural Expedition

9 Southern Ethiopia Cultural Expedition

Discover Ethiopia's most remote and culturally rich regions. Begin in Addis Ababa with visits to museums, markets, and mountain viewpoints. Journey through the Omo Valley to meet unique tribes including the Hamar, Dassanech, and Mursi. Witness the bull-jumping ceremony and explore the Kara tribe's river lifestyle. Conclude with an awe-inspiring adventure to the Danakil Depression, Lake Afdera, and the glowing lava lake of Erta Ale.

Itinerary Highlights:

  • Addis City Tour
  • Omo Valley Tribes
  • Bull Jumping & Mago Park
  • Danakil Depression, Erta Ale & Dallol
